Dis/Re Assemble

Fall 2024


Objective: Create a sculpture using only upcycled materials
Material: Sequin dress, travel size umbrella, Raw Dog, the Naked Truth of Hot Dogs, by Jamie Loftus
Process: Removed sequin mesh layer from dress and canopy of the umbrella. Replaced the canopy with Raw Dog pages, and hung from the sequin mesh covered 8" cube


Structure and Surface

Fall 2024


Objective: Create a work that plays on the concept of structure versus surface
Material: Charcoal grey merino wool, 5mm gauge wire, black spray paint, hairspray, balloons
Process: Created a black torso frame from 5mm gauge wire, assembled wool around balloons and hairsprayed in place. Removed the woolen spheres from balloons and placed on the metal frame


Untitled

Fall 2024


Objective: Sculpture 1 final project, combination of techniques used in previous works
Material: Charcoal grey merino wool,balloons, aqua-resin, silicon plaster mold, lollipop, mica powder
Process: Molded an e-cigarrete to create a pink glitter e-cigarette lollipop. Covered an aqua-resin sphere molded by a baloon in charcoal grey merino wool and placed lollipop plaster hand model.
